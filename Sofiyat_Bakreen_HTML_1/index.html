The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

Director: Peter Jackson

Genre: Fantasy / Adventure

Year Released: 2001

Awards: 4 Oscars
Fun Fact: Filmed entirely in New Zealand!

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring is an epic fantasy adventure directed by Peter Jackson.
It tells the story of Frodo Baggins, a humble hobbit who embarks on a perilous journey to destroy the One Ring and save Middle-earth.
Along the way, he is joined by a diverse fellowship of heroes united by courage and loyalty.
The movie explores themes of hope, friendship, and the battle between good and evil.
The breathtaking landscapes of New Zealand bring J.R.R. Tolkien’s world to life in stunning detail.
